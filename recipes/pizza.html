Pizza

Description

This is my favorite pizza recipe. Feel free to add whatever toppings you like or any remainders from the day before.

And of course, add plenty of cheese. Cheese makes everything better!

Ingredients

  • 450 g flour
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • 115 ml water
  • 1x Hefe
  • passierte Tomaten
  • Streukäse
  • Mozzarella
  • oregano
  • salami and other toppings

Steps

  1. Hefe in Wasser auflösen
  2. Mehl, Salz, Öl und Hefe zu einem glatten Teig vermengen
